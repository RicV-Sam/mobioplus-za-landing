How to Unsubscribe

You can cancel your MOBIO+ subscription at any time through Vodacom's official self-care channels.

Option 1: USSD (Fastest)

Dial *135*997# from your Vodacom mobile device and follow the prompts to manage and cancel your subscriptions.

Option 2: Vodacom App

1. Open the My Vodacom App.
2. Navigate to More.
3. Select Content Services.
4. View and unsubscribe from active services.

If you don’t have the app installed, search for “My Vodacom” in your phone’s app store.

Option 3: Support

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@mobixone.co.za.
